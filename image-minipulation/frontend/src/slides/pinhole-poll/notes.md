The answer is a lens.

A lens bends light rays differently depending on where and at what angle they hit the lens. With the right lens setup, you can map all rays from one scene point to one sensor point, while letting in much more light than a tiny pinhole.

A mirror would just reflect light. A larger pinhole would let in more light but would also let multiple rays from different scene points hit the same sensor location, making the image blurry. Multiple pinholes would create multiple overlapping images.
