These are some key areas where image manipulation is essential:

Medical imaging: Doctors rely on enhanced scans to find things the naked eye would miss. Think about how an X-ray might look too dark - manipulation brightens it up.

Social media filters: Every Instagram or Snapchat filter is real-time image manipulation. Face detection finds your face, then the image is transformed 30 times per second.

Foundation of CV: Every computer vision system starts by manipulating the image in some way - whether it's a self-driving car detecting lanes or a phone unlocking with your face.
