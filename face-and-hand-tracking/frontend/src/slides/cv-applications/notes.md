Traditionally, digital humans are hard to create and animate. Back in the day, movie animations had to be hand made, frame by frame and every character needed to be hand drawn.

Nowadays, modern computer vision algorithms greatly simplify this process.

For example, they can detect key points, body pose and facial expressions from videos or camera feeds and directly animate virtual characters. This could be for example from a movie-set action performance, or directly from the front camera of your phone! Directly animate emojis with your front camera.

Another application is creating your virtual characters from images of your face (we are actually working on this in our lab).
